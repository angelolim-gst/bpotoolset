        About Gusto Toolset

        This is a lightweight, all-in-one productivity tool designed for focus and efficiency.

        Core Functions:

        • Dynamic Clock: Real-time clock sync with multiple timezones.
        • Notepad & Quicklinks: A simple area for notes with templates and quick access to internal resources.
        • Visual Alarms & Timers: Set full-screen alerts for breaks, activities, and custom countdowns.
        • Calculator: With normal, formula, and time-summing modes.
        • Calendar View: A quick-glance monthly calendar with US & PH holidays.
        • Adaptive UI: Automatically adjusts its layout for small window sizes.
        • Data Portability: Export settings to a local file.

        Technology & Credits:

        • Built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (ES6).
        • Fonts 'Inter' and 'JetBrains Mono' served by Google Fonts.
        • Accurate time synchronization provided by the World Time API.
        • Formula parsing by math.js.
