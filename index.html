          About Gusto Toolset

          This is a lightweight, all-in-one productivity tool designed for focus and efficiency.

          Core Functions:

          • Dynamic Clock: Real-time clock sync with multiple timezones.
          • To-Do & Team Management: Integrated task list and automated coaching alarms.
          • Visual Alarms: Set full-screen alerts for breaks and activities.
          • Calculator: With normal, formula, and time-summing modes.
          • Calendar View: A quick-glance monthly calendar with US & PH holidays.
